# Destination Wedding Planning Checklist

Planning a destination wedding requires coordination across multiple areas. This checklist can help couples organise the major stages of the planning process.

## 12 to 18 Months Before the Wedding

* Finalise the approximate wedding date
* Decide the estimated guest count
* Establish the wedding budget
* Shortlist possible destinations
* Research and shortlist wedding venues
* Understand guest travel and accommodation requirements
* Decide whether professional wedding planning support is required

## 9 to 12 Months Before the Wedding

* Finalise the wedding venue
* Confirm the wedding planning team
* Develop the wedding design and décor direction
* Shortlist key vendors
* Begin guest list planning
* Plan the wedding functions and celebrations

## 6 to 9 Months Before the Wedding

* Finalise décor concepts
* Confirm entertainment requirements
* Finalise photography and videography
* Plan guest hospitality
* Review accommodation options
* Begin detailed event logistics planning

## 3 to 6 Months Before the Wedding

* Confirm the final guest list
* Coordinate invitations and RSVPs
* Finalise menus and catering requirements
* Confirm entertainment and performances
* Finalise transportation planning
* Review the detailed wedding timeline

## 1 to 3 Months Before the Wedding

* Confirm final guest numbers
* Finalise rooming lists
* Confirm vendor schedules
* Complete final décor discussions
* Prepare the detailed event timeline
* Confirm guest transportation
* Conduct final planning reviews

## During the Wedding

* Coordinate vendors
* Manage guest hospitality
* Monitor event timelines
* Coordinate transportation
* Manage décor and production
* Handle on-ground execution
* Resolve last-minute logistical requirements

A professional wedding planning team can help coordinate these different elements and create a smoother experience for the couple, their families and their guests.
