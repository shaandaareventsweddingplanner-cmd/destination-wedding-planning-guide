## Destination Wedding Planning

A successful destination wedding requires detailed coordination across multiple areas, including venue selection, accommodation, travel logistics, guest experience, décor, entertainment and vendor management.

The planning process may include:

1. Understanding the couple's vision and requirements
2. Shortlisting suitable wedding destinations and venues
3. Developing the wedding design and décor concept
4. Coordinating wedding vendors
5. Managing guest accommodation and hospitality
6. Planning transportation and event logistics
7. Coordinating wedding functions and entertainment
8. Managing on-ground execution
